Description: The 2026 Bajaj Pulsar NS125 is an entry-level streetfighter that brings the aggressive design language of its bigger siblings (NS200) to the 125cc segment. It is powered by a 124.45cc, 4-valve, air-cooled DTS-i engine producing 12 PS. For 2026, it receives significant updates including a fully digital instrument cluster with Bluetooth connectivity (call alerts, turn-by-turn navigation) and, on the top variant, selectable ABS modes (Rain, Road, Off-Road) . It is known for its muscular tank, perimeter frame (rare in this class), and stable high-speed handling, making it a favorite among young riders looking for style and performance on a budget .
